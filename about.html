Welcome!

Docendo discimus (Latin for "By teaching, we learn.")

I'm currently a second year PhD student studying bioinformatics and computational biology at Oregon Health & Science University. My research interests broadly span integrative genomics, metagenomics and the human gut microbiome, translational bioinformatics, and the use of high-performance computing in genomics research and applications.

Previously, I was at the University of Oklahoma where I received dual Bachelor degrees in biochemistry and mathematics.

In my free time, I like to play tennis, do calligraphy, fold origami, and learn foreign languages.
